1. Risk Management Fundamentals üîë

What is Risk?

An uncertain event that, if it occurs, has a positive or negative effect on project objectives.

Threats vs Opportunities

Threats: Reduce value
Opportunities: Increase value

PMI Lens: Treat opportunities with the same rigor as threats.

Risk Owner

The person responsible for monitoring triggers and executing responses.

2. Risk Planning Overview
